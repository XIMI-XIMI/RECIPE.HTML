SANDWICH

Chicken Cheeseteaks Sandwich

Prepare Time : 10 minutes

Cook Time : 10 minutes

Total Time : 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 onion sliced thin
  • 2 tablespoons olive or vegetable oil
  • 3 bell peppers (red and green), chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 medium chicken breasts cut into thin 1/4'' slices
  • 1 teaspoon creole Italian or taco seasoning
  • 6 slices provolone cheese
  • 4 hoagie rolls
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup light mayo or sour-cream

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large skillet, heat about 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat, add bell pepper and onions.
  2. Cook until soft, stirring occasionally, 4-5 minutes.
  3. Remove from pan onto a clean plate.
  4. Add 1 more tablespoon of oil to the same pan and heat.
  5. Stir in chicken and seasoning.
  6. Cook until chicken is cooked through and browned, 4-5 minutes.
  7. Return peppers to pan and mix to combine.
  8. Spread mayo inside sub rolls and fill with the chicken and pepper mixture.
  9. Top each sub with 2 slices of cheese and broil in the oven until the cheese is melted and gooey. Enjoy!
